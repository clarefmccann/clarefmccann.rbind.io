Hi, welcome to my website! I'm a Ph.D. candidate in Developmental Psychology at the University of California, Los Angeles. I work with Dr. Jennifer Silvers in the [Social Affective Neuroscience and Development lab](https://silverslab.psych.ucla.edu/). I’m interested in understanding adolescence, a time in life when a lot is changing all at once. The brain is developing rapidly, puberty is underway, and social and emotional challenges are becoming more complex.
I am working to build models that help track these changes over time, with the goal of understanding how they work together to shape teens’ mental health, behavior, and overall well-being.

Read more about my research interests in [my CV](/cv/).

I also enjoy reading (my goodreads is somewhere on here)! And learning new methods-y things!
